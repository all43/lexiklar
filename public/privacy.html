Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Lexiklar is a fully offline German dictionary app. This policy describes what data the app handles and how.

Data stored on your device

Lexiklar stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Search history — recently visited and frequently viewed words
  • Favorites — words you have saved
  • Preferences — theme, language, and display settings

This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server. You can clear it at any time in Settings.

No account required

Lexiklar does not require an account, login, or any personal information to use.

No analytics or advertising

The app contains no analytics, crash reporting, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking of any kind.

Dictionary updates

The app includes a voluntary "Check for updates" feature in Settings. When you use it, the app makes a network request to GitHub servers to check for a new dictionary version. This request may cause your IP address to be logged by GitHub as part of their standard server logging. No personal data is sent by Lexiklar in this request. This feature is entirely opt-in and the app functions fully without it.

Dictionary data

The dictionary content is bundled with the app and stored entirely on your device. It is derived from German Wiktionary (CC BY-SA 4.0) and frequency corpora. No user data is associated with or transmitted alongside this data.
