Grammar reference, shareable links, and accessibility improvements.

Grammar reference
- New Grammar section in Settings with 8 topic pages: noun gender rules, adjective declension, determiners, cases & prepositions, modal verbs, reflexive verbs, connectors, and verb tenses (Zeitformen)
- Each page links directly to relevant dictionary entries
- Cases page includes a visual diagram of case relationships

Shareable links
- Tap the share button on any word, sense, or grammar page to copy a direct link
- Links open to the exact word, sense number, or grammar section

Accessibility
- Gender colors now meet WCAG AA contrast (≥ 5:1) in both light and dark mode
- New High Contrast mode in Settings for even stronger color separation
- Screen reader support: ARIA labels on gender badges, column and row headers on all declension tables
