# Store Listing — Tone & Guidelines

## Voice
- Confident, not promotional
- Direct — lead with what makes it different, not generic praise
- No hype words (amazing, powerful, best)

## Origin story
Lexiklar was built during a personal German learning journey. The core frustration: dictionaries tell you what a word means but not how to use it — articles, case endings, conjugation tables scattered across multiple sources. Always include this motivation in the "Built for learners" / "Für Lernende gemacht" section.

## What to avoid
- Never mention "beta" in any store copy
- No "initial release" framing that signals immaturity
- Don't undersell: this is a finished, complete app

## Key differentiators (always mention)
- Fully offline
- Grammar depth: declensions, conjugations, gender rules
- No ads, no account, no tracking
- English ↔ German search
